How It Works

Three steps.
A lifetime of meaning.

01

Write your letter

Pour your heart onto the page. Write to yourself five years from now, to your child on their wedding day, or to a friend who needs to hear your words at just the right moment.

02

Choose the moment

Pick an exact date — a birthday, an anniversary, a decade from today. Your letter stays sealed in our vault, untouched, until that day arrives.

03

It arrives — perfectly

On the chosen date, Futurely delivers your letter exactly as written. No edits, no fading memory — just your words, arriving like a gift from the past.
